Almond Sweets Recipe

Introduction

This delicious desert can be served with Tea or coffee. This recipe produces 50 sweets.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 100ml Honey
  • 500g Almonds

Steps

  1. Reheat the oven at 150℃
  2. Reduce almond to powder
  3. Add honey and the egg white
  4. form small balls of 2cm
  5. Back for 13min
